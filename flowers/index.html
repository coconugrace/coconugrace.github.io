Before we continue, I find it necessary to explain the purpose of flowers.

There many many different kinds of flowers out there, all created for the same general purpose.

In botany, flowers are more than simply beautiful works of art. Flowers are essential for a plant's reproduction. The sperm, containing pollen, and the ovule meet in the ovary, in the center of the flower.
